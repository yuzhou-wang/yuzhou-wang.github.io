Yeshu's current research focuses on developing an AI Assistant for air quality that 
parses natural language queries against EPA emissions datasets, mapping locations to 
spatial grid cells and running dispersion models to provide actionable insights into 
pollution sources and regional health impacts. She has previously worked on critic-guided 
agentic Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines for multi-hop reasoning, 
domain-adapted NLP systems for complex, unstructured clinical data, and automated 
evaluation frameworks for LLM prompt optimization.
